How to Brew

Basic Coffee Brewing

  1. Measure coffee

    2. 1–2 tablespoons of coffee powder per cup (180–200 ml).

  2. Heat water

    3. Ideal temperature: 90–96°C (just before boiling).

  3. Add coffee to water

    4. Depends on the brewing method (filter, French press, etc.).

  4. Brew

    5. Let water extract flavor from the coffee grounds.

  5. Serve

    6. Pour and enjoy plain or with milk/sugar.

Common Coffee Brewing Methods

  1. Filter Coffee (Drip Method)

    2. Best for beginners

    Steps:

    1. Place filter paper in the coffee filter.
    2. Add medium-ground coffee.
    3. Pour hot water slowly over the coffee.
    4. Coffee drips into the pot below.

      5. Time: 3-5 minutes

      Taste: Clean and smooth

  2. French Press

    3. Rich and strong

    Steps:

    1. Add coarse coffee grounds to the French press.
    2. Pour hot water.
    3. Stir and cover.
    4. Wait 4 minutes.
    5. Press the plunger down slowly.

      6. Time: 4-5 minutes

      Taste: Full-bodied

  3. Espresso (Machine)

    4. Strong and concentrated

    Steps:

    1. Use finely ground coffee.
    2. Tamp coffee in the portafilter.
    3. Brew under high pressure.

      4. Time: 25-30 seconds

      Taste: Intense,bold

  4. South Indian Filter Coffee

    5. Traditional method

    Steps:

    1. Add coffee powder to the filter.
    2. Pour boiling water.
    3. Let it drip slowly.
    4. Mix decoction with hot milk and sugar.

      5. Taste: strong,aromatic

  5. Cold Brew

    6. Smooth and less acidic

    Steps:

    1. Mix coarse coffee grounds with cold water.
    2. Cover and keep for 12–24 hours.
    3. Strain and serve over ice.

      4. Taste: Mild,smooth

Coffee Terminology

Espresso
Coffee Base
Strong coffee brewed under high pressure.
Base for Cappachino and other drinks.
Cappuccino
Coffee Drink
Espresso with steamed milk and milk foam.
Typically served in morning.
Latte
Coffee Drink
Espresso mixed with plenty of steamed milk.
Macchiato
Coffee Drink
Espresso topped with a small amount of milk or foam.
Cold Brew
Brewing Method
Coffee brewed with cold water for a long duration.
Crema
Coffee Layer
Golden foam layer on top of espresso.
Barista
Service Personnel
A trained person who prepares and serves coffee.
